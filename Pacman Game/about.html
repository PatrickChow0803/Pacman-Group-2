About Game

Player has three lives by default. The goal of the game is to accumulate as many points as possible by collecting dots, fruits, and eating blue ghosts, while avoiding the trustee ghosts. When all of the dots in a stage are eaten, that stage is completed, and the player will advance to the next level. There will be three levels in total. One life is lost when Pac-Man is caught by a trustee. If all three lives are lost, it is game over.
